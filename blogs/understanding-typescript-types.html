Understanding TypeScript Types

2025-10-10

At first, TypeScript felt like extra work — but it’s actually a safety net. By defining types, you catch bugs before they ever make it to the browser. It’s like having a super strict friend who keeps you from making dumb mistakes.

Types also make your code cleaner and easier to maintain. Once you get used to it, writing JavaScript without TypeScript feels like walking a tightrope without a net.
